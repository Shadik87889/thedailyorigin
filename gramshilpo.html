EVERYTHING A CHAIRMAN NEEDS.

GramShilpo provides a complete toolkit to run your Union Parishad or village administration efficiently.

Holding Management

Track every household, holding number, and owner details. Simplifies tax collection and census data.

Population Database

A searchable database of all villagers with NID, mobile numbers, and occupation details.

Bongsho (Lineage)

Unique feature to map family trees and lineages within the village, preserving local history.

Volunteer Network

Categorized list of social workers, blood donors, and educators ready to help the community.

Digital Notices

Publish official notices regarding vaccination camps, relief distribution, or meetings instantly.

Demographic Stats

Real-time graphs showing age distribution (Child, Adult, Senior) to help in planning development projects.

Emergency Contacts

Direct access to Chairman, Members, and Village Police contact numbers for citizens.

Certificate Issue

Streamline the process of issuing birth, character, and residency certificates (Citizenship).

Village Court

Manage village court cases, dates, and resolutions digitally.
