SportShilpo is a complete toolkit for organizers, scorers, and academies.

Admin Dashboard

Your central command center to create tournaments, assign teams, and schedule matches instantly.

Mobile Scoring App

Scorers can use any smartphone to update runs, wickets, and extras. Works seamlessly offline.

Team Registration

Allow teams to register online. Collect player details, photos, and roles automatically.

Points Table

Auto-calculating points table with Net Run Rate (NRR), Wins, Losses, and Ties updated live.
